Collaboration and Podio

1st Revenue is all about collaboration. We use an excellent Podio platform that provides great programming API to seamlessly integrate business model functionality with the native Podio capabilities and rich Podio app ecosystem. Every change you do in your model is visible through native Podio interface. Model can become a part of much larger collaboration workflow.
When more than one user opens the same model and they start making changes, such changes are propagated to all the connected users, be it simple shift of the position after dragging the sticker on the screen or modification of the sticker fields or color. The changes are sent to our backend which uses real time technology to distribute them to others.
Even for a lone user the collaboration features may be very useful. Each of us has more than one connected device and most of them can be used to access 1st Revenue model. You may have two screens in front of you. Open separate browser windows, connect both to 1st Revenue, then display the Canvas full screen on one of them while editing stickers on another. Use a projector to show your model to audience. The changes will instantly appear on both of them, just like they will do if you make changes on your tablet or even use Podio application on your iPhone and edit the fields in any of the blocks. 3 or 4 devices may work in unison.

Platforms and browsers

1st Revenue works on multiple platforms and browsers. We don't however recommend Internet Explorer since we haven't tested 1st Revenue with it and it's known to be slower and incompatible with the latest web standards that we have used to build 1st Revenue. We test 1st Revenue with Chrome and Firefox on Windows, Linux, and Mac OS X and with Safari on Mac OS X. For tablet testing we use iPad with Safari and Chrome.
The recommended screen resolution should be at least 960x600. This fits most of the desktop and tablet screens, including iPad, which we are testing as a reference platform for tablets.
Note that on the desktop browsers you can resize the screen whenever you like and the screen elements will adapt dynamically to fit the new size. We still are working on the best proportions of different element sizes for each zoom level. Please send us your feedback if your specific window size or platform displays the information incorrectly or at inconvenient size.

Full screen mode

On desktop browsers like Chrome or Firefox clicking key F11 on the keyboard switches 1st Revenue to full screen mode. 1st Revenue takes over all screen space and provides much larger work area. Combine it with Zoom level 4 (Single block mode) and you have entire screen for your stickers. Try other zoom levels to see the sticker layout that's best for for you.
1st Revenue icon can be saved to one of the iPad Home screens and then it will operate in full screen mode without any browser specific decorations. To create an icon on Home screen, open 1st Revenue in iPad Safari, navigate to Models menu and click on the icon on toolbar left from the URL that looks like a rectangle with an arrow pointing out of it . Click on this icon and choose the option "Add to Home Screen". Note that currently 1st Revenue closes down in most cases after iPad enters power save mode and locks the screen. It's a good choice when working non-stop for prolonged periods of time however the work may be interrupted after a timeout and app should start again. For short interactions use Safari or Chrome on iPad directly.
